# Learning Paths

## Maintenance Core Skills (4-week preview)
Week 1: Tools + safety fundamentals  
Week 2: Plumbing basics (toilets, faucets, drains)  
Week 3: Electrical basics (outlets, switches, breakers)  
Week 4: HVAC basics + troubleshooting (thermostats, filters, airflow)

Next: expand into Turns + Repairs, Appliances, and On-call basics.
